Identity V (or IDV for short) is an asymmetrical horror game developed by Netease where you have 4 survivors and one hunter. If you've played or heard of Dead By Daylight, it's basically that, but more silly in apperance and if everyone were instead dolls.

As a survivor, you try to complete 5 ciphers and open the exit gates to escape, ideally with as much of your team alive as possible.

The hunter is the guy that kills you.

Simple enough, right?
